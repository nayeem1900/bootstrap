Permit Expediting A Permit Expediter is a trained professional that advises clients throughout the permitting process to keep construction projects on schedule and within budget. Permit Expediters typically have backgrounds in Planning, Construction, Real Estate, or Architecture and have experience working with local compliance codes and zoning requirements.
